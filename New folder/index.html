About

Designing with purpose
and precision

I'm Viddittha Cheithan, a UI/UX Designer and BCA graduate from Vidya First Grade College, Tumkur University. I believe that great design sits at the intersection of empathy and logic — understanding users deeply, then solving their problems beautifully.

My work at Zidio Development gave me hands-on experience across the full design pipeline: from discovery and competitor analysis to high-fidelity prototypes and developer handoffs. I thrive in collaborative environments and care deeply about visual consistency and usability.

I'm fluent in Tamil, Kannada, and English, and I bring the same multi-layered thinking to my design work — always considering context, culture, and the human behind the screen.
